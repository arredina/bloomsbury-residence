About

We are building a dedicated technology residence in the High Atlas — a place where ambitious builders live, think, and create alongside each other, removed from distraction, surrounded by silence and altitude.

Accommodation

Where will we
be staying

High Atlas Organic House — an authentic Berber guesthouse renovated in the Western High Atlas, near Toubkal. Sleeping up to 20, it offers walking trails, total quiet, and views over the valley.
